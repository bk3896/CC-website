About

A studio built for

Clubhouse Creative is a Long Beach photography and video studio working with businesses, teams, and organizations across Southern California.

The Studio

Photography and video
with intention.

Clubhouse Creative was founded to fill a gap between overpriced agency work and generic freelance photography. We create polished, usable visual content for companies that need to look sharp without overcomplicating the process.

Every shoot is planned around your actual needs — the website you are building, the event you are hosting, the team you are growing. We show up prepared, work efficiently, and deliver work you will use for years.

Based in Long Beach, we cover all of Southern California and travel nationally for the right projects.

Who We Work With

Modern brands,
growing teams.

We work best with companies that take their visual identity seriously. That includes startups building their first brand assets, established businesses refreshing their look, and organizations that need reliable coverage for events and campaigns.

Our clients include corporate teams, hospitality brands, golf and country clubs, conferences, nonprofits, and founders building personal brands. If you need images that perform across websites, social, print, and pitch decks — that is what we do.

What We Value

Three things that
guide every project.

Clarity

No bloated proposals or vague timelines. We scope every project clearly so you know exactly what you are getting, when, and at what cost before we start.

Craft

Good lighting, clean composition, intentional retouching. The details matter because your audience will notice, even if they cannot articulate why.

Consistency

Whether it is 5 headshots or 500 event photos, the quality stays the same. That reliability is what keeps clients coming back season after season.
