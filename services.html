Questions

Before you reach out.

Where are you based?
Long Beach, California. We cover all of Southern California and travel for the right projects.
How far in advance should I book?
Two to three weeks is ideal for most projects. For large events or multi-day shoots, a month or more helps with planning.
What does a typical project cost?
Headshot sessions start at $250. Brand content, event coverage, and booth activations are scoped per project based on deliverables, duration, and location.
How fast do I get my images?
Headshots are delivered within 48 hours. Event galleries within one week. Brand content projects within 5 to 7 business days. Rush delivery is available.
Do you offer video as well?
Yes. Short-form video, reels, and behind-the-scenes content are part of our brand content service. Longer-form video is available as a custom scope.
What usage rights are included?
All projects include full commercial usage rights for your business. Extended licensing for advertising or third-party use can be discussed during scoping.
