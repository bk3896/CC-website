Palos Verdes Golf Club's 2024 LPGA stop brought elite professional golf to one of Southern California's most distinctive venues — a Spanish Colonial clubhouse perched above the Pacific, fairways lined with eucalyptus and gallery crowds. Coverage spanned practice days through the final round: range sessions, sponsor activations, on-course action, 18th-hole drama, and the full trophy ceremony.
Deliverables were tailored for club communications, member magazines, sponsor decks, and social media, balancing editorial storytelling with clean brand-forward imagery.
